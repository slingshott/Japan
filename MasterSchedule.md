## Potential Issues & Major Misses

**Luggage at Himeji:** Ship your large bags from Kyoto to Hiroshima the day before (Mar 27). Coin lockers at Himeji Station will be full during Cherry Blossom season.

**Cherry Blossom Timing:** You'll hit the start of the season. Add Shinjuku Gyoen (Tokyo) and Maruyama Park (Kyoto) to walking routes for early-blooming weeping cherry trees.

**Oversized Baggage Seats:** Reserve the "Oversized Baggage" seats at the back of Shinkansen cars if bringing large suitcases. Luggage forwarding between cities makes this a non-issue.
